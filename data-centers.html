Industry

Data Centers

Precision thermal management for high-density server environments where a single degree of variance can trigger cascade failure.

45°C+ Ambient tolerance in high-density rack environments
24/7 Continuous load rating — no derating at full utilisation
Keeping compute online when heat is the threat
Enquire for This Industry

Data centers operate at the intersection of maximum electrical density and zero tolerance for thermal excursion. Modern GPU clusters and AI inference racks generate heat loads exceeding 50kW per rack — far beyond what standard CRAC units were designed to handle. Ambient temperatures in poorly managed facilities climb fast, and when they do, throttling kicks in before hardware fails. The result is degraded performance, missed SLAs, and unplanned downtime that costs operators thousands per minute.

TMG supplies aftermarket radiators, charge air coolers, and hydraulic oil coolers purpose-built for the cooling infrastructure that keeps data centers running. Our components are used in generator sets providing backup power, in liquid cooling distribution units, and in the HVAC plant that maintains ambient conditions across the floor. Every unit ships pressure-tested and ready to install — no lead time negotiation, no distributor markup.

Where generic suppliers offer catalogue parts with 6–8 week lead times, TMG holds US-based inventory across the most common data center cooling configurations. Our engineering team has matched radiator cores to Cummins, Caterpillar, and Kohler generator sets used in Tier III and Tier IV facilities. When a cooling component fails at 2am, we're the call that gets the part on-site before the next business day.
