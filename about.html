The TMG Story

About Thermal Motion Group

Thermal Motion Group was founded on a single premise: industrial operators deserve better than generic cooling solutions. We don't just sell radiators; we engineer thermal management ecosystems that keep heavy industry moving in the world's most hostile environments.

From high-density data centers to utility-scale solar farms, our components are the silent partners in mission-critical infrastructure. With a legacy rooted in aftermarket excellence, we have expanded into a multi-divisional power house, covering everything from battery storage cooling to precision metal fabrication.

Our approach is data-driven and spec-heavy. We understand the cost of downtime, which is why every TMG product is built to exceed OEM standards for durability, pressure rating, and thermal efficiency.
