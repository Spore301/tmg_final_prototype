# Thermal Motion Group (TMG) - Screens Knowledge Base

This document serves as an inventory of all the fully designed high-fidelity screens available in the `phase-2-full` directory. These screens represent the complete set of pages built so far, adhering to the "Industrial Architect" design system.

## Core Navigation & Discovery Pages

*   **`index.html`**
    *   **Title:** TMG Homepage Responsive
    *   **Purpose:** The main entry point to the website. Features hero section, quick product categories, industry applications, newsletter signup, and corporate overview.
*   **`search.html`**
    *   **Title:** Search — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** Dedicated search results page for global site search queries (e.g., searching for parts, news, or solutions).

## E-Commerce & Product Catalog

*   **`products.html`**
    *   **Title:** TMG Products Responsive
    *   **Purpose:** The main Product Listing Page (PLP) or Product Catalog. Features grid layouts of product categories, filters, and high-level product discovery.
*   **`aftermarket-radiators.html`**
    *   **Title:** Aftermarket Radiators | TMG
    *   **Purpose:** A specific sub-category or product family page. Details a specialized segment of products under the TMG brand.
*   **`pdp.html`**
    *   **Title:** Heavy-Duty Aluminium Radiator — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** The Product Detail Page (PDP). Provides full specifications, imagery, pricing/quote CTA, and technical documentation for a single product.

## Conversion & User Flow

*   **`cart.html`**
    *   **Title:** Quote Cart — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** The shopping cart or "quote cart" page where users review their selected items before submitting an RFQ (Request for Quote) or checking out.
*   **`cart-drawer-snippet.html`**
    *   **Purpose:** A partial HTML snippet representing the off-canvas/flyout cart drawer (used for quick cart review without leaving the current page).
*   **`auth.html`**
    *   **Title:** Sign In / Sign Up — Thermal Motion Group
    *   **Purpose:** The authentication hub handling user login, registration, and potentially password recovery flows.

## Corporate & Informational Pages

*   **`about.html`**
    *   **Title:** About Us — Thermal Motion Group
    *   **Purpose:** Corporate profile page covering TMG's history, mission, global presence, and values.
*   **`contact.html`**
    *   **Title:** Contact Us — Thermal Motion Group
    *   **Purpose:** The primary contact point containing the inquiry form, global office locations, and support channels.
*   **`news.html`**
    *   **Title:** News & Resources — Thermal Motion Group
    *   **Purpose:** The blog/news listing page featuring press releases, technical articles, and company updates in a grid format.

## Expertise Hubs

*   **`industries.html`**
    *   **Title:** Industries We Serve — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** The main hub showcasing the various industrial sectors TMG provides cooling solutions for.
*   **`data-centers.html`**
    *   **Title:** Data Centers — TMG Industries
    *   **Purpose:** A detailed example of an individual industry page, focusing on solutions specifically tailored for Data Centers.
*   **`applications.html`**
    *   **Title:** Applications — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** Details how TMG products are applied in real-world scenarios across different machinery or processes.
*   **`solutions.html`**
    *   **Title:** Solutions — TMG
    *   **Purpose:** Highlights comprehensive engineering and custom manufacturing services offered beyond standard catalog products.
