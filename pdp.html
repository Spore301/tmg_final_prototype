Product Overview
Aluminium bar-and-plate core engineered for high-dust, high-vibration agricultural and earthmoving environments. Pressure-tested to 10 bar. Ships from US stock.
24-month warranty · US stock · No lag

The TMG Heavy-Duty Aluminium Radiator is engineered for agricultural and earthmoving equipment operating in high-dust, high-vibration environments. Built with an aluminium bar-and-plate core, it delivers superior thermal performance over OEM copper-brass units while reducing weight by up to 30%. Direct-fit replacement for John Deere 8R, Caterpillar 320D, and Komatsu PC200 platforms. All units are pressure-tested to 10 bar before dispatch and ship from US warehouse stock. No custom lead time on standard variants.
